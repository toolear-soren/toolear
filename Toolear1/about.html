About Toolear

Toolear is a collection of simple, fast and free online tools.

Our goal is to save people time on routine tasks: formatting text and JSON, converting files, generating small snippets, and doing everyday calculations. Instead of installing heavy apps or searching dozens of sites, users can open Toolear, find a tool in seconds, use it, and move on with their work.

Key principles of Toolear:

  • Free access – all current tools are available without registration or payment.
  • Speed and simplicity – minimum clicks, clear interface, fast results.
  • Privacy – we do not store the data you paste into tools. All processing happens in the browser where technically possible.

Toolear is an independent project that is actively evolving. New tools are being added over time, and existing ones are improved based on real user needs.
