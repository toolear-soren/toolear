Frequently Asked Questions

Is Toolear free to use?
Yes, all tools on Toolear are 100% free. We support the project through advertising, so you don't have to pay.
Do you save my files or data?
No. Most of our tools (like JSON Formatter, Image Compressor) run directly in your browser. Your files and text do not leave your computer. We prioritize your privacy.
Why isn't a tool working for me?
Please make sure you are using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari) and that JavaScript is enabled. If the problem persists, try disabling ad blockers as they sometimes interfere with tool scripts.
Can I suggest a new tool?
Absolutely! We are always looking to expand. Please email us at toolear.contact@gmail.com with your suggestion.
