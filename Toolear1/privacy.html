Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how Toolear (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses and protects information when you use the website toolear.space and our tools.

1. Who we are

Toolear is a website that provides free online tools (converters, formatters, calculators and similar utilities). If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: toolear.contact@gmail.com

2. What data we collect

We do not ask you to create an account and we do not collect your name, address or phone number.

We may collect:

  • Basic technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system.
  • Usage data: which pages and tools you open, approximate time on page, clicks.

This data is collected through standard web server logs and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or similar services).

3. Data you enter into tools

Many tools on Toolear allow you to paste text, numbers or upload small files.

  • Client-side processing: Where technically possible, processing happens directly in your browser using JavaScript.
  • No storage: We do not store the content you paste into tools on our servers for long-term use.
  • No profiling: We do not use your tool input to build user profiles.

4. Cookies and third-party services

We use cookies to remember basic preferences (language, theme) and measure anonymous statistics.

Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses data here: Google Ads Privacy.

5. Legal basis and purpose

We process technical and usage data to operate, maintain, and protect the website from abuse and attacks.
